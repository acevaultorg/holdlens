How accelerators are computed

Aggregate net flow, walked forward

  • 1. Per-quarter net delta. For every ticker and every tracked quarter, count smart-money new + add actions and subtract trim + exit actions. Positive means the fleet net-added that quarter.
  • 2. Longest positive run. Walk the 8-quarter series oldest → newest and find the longest contiguous run of strictly positive deltas. Ties break toward the most recent run.
  • 3. Flag 3Q+. Runs of 2 quarters are listed but not emphasized. Runs of 3+ quarters are accelerators — distinct from /crowded-trades (ownership snapshot) and /trend-streak (per-manager streak).

The sparkline band in the per-quarter section shades the run itself in solid emerald; quarters outside the run are faint. The window runs from 2024-Q1 through the latest filed quarter.

Not investment advice. 13F filings are delayed 45 days and report long-only positions. Methodology.
