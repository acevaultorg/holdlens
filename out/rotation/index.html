Why this beats Dataroma

  • Rotation, not snapshot. Dataroma shows one quarter's sector breakdown. HoldLens shows 8 quarters side-by-side so you can see the direction smart money is moving, not just where they stand.
  • Size-weighted flow. Each move is weighted by the position's portfolio impact — a 20% bet counts for 3× a 2% nibble — so the heatmap reflects conviction, not just move count.
  • Hover for the detail. Every cell shows the exact buy count, sell count, and net score on hover.
  • Free, forever. Dataroma paywalls their sector views. HoldLens doesn't.
