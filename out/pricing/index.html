Pricing FAQ

Is the free tier really free forever?
Yes. The core 13F tracker — recommendation model, multi-quarter trends, live prices, signal dossiers, activity feed, screener, compare, watchlist — stays free for everyone. Pro adds email alerts, conviction scoring, alpha attribution, and API access.
What's the founders price?
Sign up for early access today and you lock in $14/mo for life. After Q2 2026 launch, the price goes up to $19/mo for new subscribers. Annual ($140/yr) saves you another $28.
When does Pro launch?
Q2 2026 — once we ship the EDGAR parser, Resend integration, and Stripe. Email signups today get notified the day Pro goes live.
Why $14/mo and not $9 or $29?
Below $10 it feels disposable, above $20 it requires justification. $14 is the sweet spot for an individual investor tool: about half a coffee per week. You'll save that on your first informed move.
Do you offer a team or family plan?
Not yet. If you want to share Pro with your investing club or family, reach out — we'll set something up manually until we ship a real team plan.
Refund policy?
Cancel anytime. Pro-rated refund within 14 days, no questions asked. After 14 days, your subscription runs to the end of the period and then stops.
