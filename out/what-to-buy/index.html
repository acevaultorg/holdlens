How HoldLens decides what to buy

We track every 13F filing from 30 of the best portfolio managers in the world — the ones with multi-decade track records and measurable alpha. Every buy and sell they report is scored by our multi-factor recommendation model: manager quality (70%), consensus across managers (20%), fresh-money share (10%), and a concentration bonus when a buyer commits over 10% of their portfolio. Multi-quarter trend streaks get additional weight — if three Tier-1 managers have been adding for three consecutive quarters, that's a much stronger signal than any single move.
