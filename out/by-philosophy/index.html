How schools are assigned

Mutually exclusive, hand-classified

  • 1. Philosophy strings. Each tracked manager has a short philosophy line on record. We read it and place the manager in exactly one school.
  • 2. Aggregate top holdings. Within each school, sum every member’s top-holding pct across tickers. The ticker with the highest summed weight is the school’s collective top pick.
  • 3. Show top 10 per school. Sorted by summed weight desc, tiebroken by owner count and ConvictionScore.

A manager can only belong to one school. Some edge cases (e.g., Stephen Mandel, a Tiger Cub who runs long-only GARP) get placed where the track-record fit is cleanest. The classification is curated, not automatic.

Not investment advice. School assignments reflect stated philosophy and track-record fit; managers may disagree with their placement. Methodology.
